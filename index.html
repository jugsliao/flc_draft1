About us

Fig Learning Centre (FIG) was established in 1997 as a high-quality academic centre to support its students in meeting their future career aspirations. We began operation in Katong (Singapore) and relocated to the present Waterloo Centre in 2002, which is equipped with a Science laboratory to promote hands-on activities. ​ Through our result-oriented teaching methods, many batches of students achieved excellent exam scores that enabled them to transit successfully to higher-level education. Presently, our focus is on providing tutoring service (group or individual) to pre-university students in Math and Science-related subjects. Apart from ensuring a good foundation is laid on the concepts, we also strive to deepen the learning process by going beyond the subject syllabus requirements. We would be happy to work with you in supporting your academic aspirations. Please feel free to contact us for a discussion on providing you with a suitable learning program.
